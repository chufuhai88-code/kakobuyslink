How to read a row without mistaking hype for proof

Every author formats differently, yet the same ingredients keep showing up: a link, a slangy batch callout, maybe a seller snap, and a guessy price that forgets duty, insurance, and box size. Until Kakobuy attaches warehouse frames, every field is just a signpost.

  • permalink vs shop front: agents want the deep link to the variant you meant; hero URLs love to break grids.
  • Friendly batch names: handy mnemonics, not contracts—note the date you copied them.
  • tiny JPEG “QC”: marketing collateral until your agent retrains the lens on your unit.
  • Column dollars or yuan: ballpark only; rebuild landed cost inside Kakobuy.
  • Tabs and lanes: if a shoe row “disappeared,” assume the seller rewrote the album, not the universe.

FAQ

Strip the marketing: what’s a Kakobuy spreadsheet really?

A crowd-routed list of Chinese marketplace URLs you paste into Kakobuy. We narrate; the browse UI you launch from our buttons shows what’s live today; Kakobuy settles the bill.

If the row has QC pics, am I done?

Not until your agent’s warehouse set matches what you expect. Seller JPEGs are previews, not rulings.

Why did yesterday’s link evaporate?

Shops constantly retitle, merge, or sunset SKUs. Reload through browse instead of assuming someone “deleted your grail on purpose.”

Are you “official” Kakobuy?

No—Kakobuyslink is its own notes site. Commerce and policy live with Kakobuy at kakobuy.com.

Can I live off Discord screenshots?

They’re the fastest-aging artifact in the loop. Pair them with the home checklist and Guides when something expensive is in the box.
